# Discovery of Pluto (1930)

Pluto was discovered on 18 February 1930 by Clyde Tombaugh at the Lowell
Observatory in Flagstaff, Arizona. Following its discovery it was announced
as the ninth planet of the Solar System. It was named Pluto in 1930, a name
suggested by Venetia Burney, an 11-year-old schoolgirl in Oxford, England.
